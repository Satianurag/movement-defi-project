## üí∞ DeFi (Decentralized Finance) Kya Hai?

Traditional banking ke bina paise invest karna, interest kamana, ya loan lena. Sab kuch **smart contracts** se automated hai.

### Key Concept: **Smart Contracts**
Self-executing code jo blockchain par chalta hai. Jaise vending machine - paisa dalo, automatic product milta hai. Koi middleman nahi.

---

## üìä Important Terms

### 1. **TVL (Total Value Locked)**
Protocol mein kitna paisa invested hai.
- **Example**: Canopy ka TVL = $36.9M (36.9 million dollars worth tokens locked)

### 2. **APY (Annual Percentage Yield)**
Aapke paiso par saal mein kitna return milega (interest).
- **Example**: 14.34% APY = ‚Çπ100 invest karo, 1 saal baad ‚Çπ114.34 milenge

### 3. **APR (Annual Percentage Rate)**
APY jaisa hi, but compounding ke bina (simple interest).

### 4. **Wallet**
Aapka digital bank account. Example: MetaMask, Phantom.
- **Address**: Aapka account number (0x123abc...)
- **Private Key**: Password (NEVER share!)

### 5. **Token**
Digital currency. Jaise dollar, euro - blockchain par.
- **MOVE**: Movement ka native token
- **USDC, USDT**: Dollar-pegged stablecoins ($1 = 1 USDC)
- **LP Token**: Liquidity pool mein paisa dalne ka proof

## üè¶ Movement Network Par Platforms

### 1. **Native Staking** (Direct MOVE Staking)
**Kya Hai:** Apne $MOVE tokens "lock" karo, network security ke liye kamao.
- **APY:** ~10% (validator commission ke baad)
- **Risk:** Low (network khud manage karta hai)
- **Example:** 1000 MOVE stake karo ‚Üí 100 MOVE/year kamao

---

### 2. **Canopy Hub** (Yield Aggregator)
**Kya Hai:** Automatic strategy jo best APY dhundti hai.
- **Working:** Aap USDC deposit karo ‚Üí Canopy automatically sabse best jagah invest karega
- **APY:** 4-14% (strategy pe depend)
- **Fees:** 1.5% management fee yearly
- **Example:** USDC-sUSDa vault = 14.34% APY

**Analogy:** Mutual fund manager jo aapke liye best schemes dhundta hai.

---

### 3. **Echelon Market** (Lending Protocol)
**Kya Hai:** Crypto bank - paisa deposit karo (earning) ya loan lo (borrow).

#### Supply (Lend):
- Apne tokens deposit karo ‚Üí Interest kamao
- **Example:** 1000 USDC deposit ‚Üí 4% APY

#### Borrow:
- Collateral rakho, loan lo
- **Example:** 1 ETH collateral ‚Üí 700 USDC loan (70% LTV)

**Terms:**
- **LTV (Loan-to-Value):** Kitna % loan mil sakta hai
- **Liquidation:** Agar collateral value gir gaya, tumhara collateral bech diya jayega

---

### 4. **Meridian AMM** (Decentralized Exchange - DEX)
**Kya Hai:** Token swap karo bina central exchange (Binance, Coinbase).

#### Swap:
- MOVE ‚Üí USDC seedha exchange karo
- **Fee:** 0.3% per trade

#### Liquidity Provider (LP):
- Dono tokens pool mein dalo (e.g., MOVE + USDC)
- Trading fees se kamao
- **APY:** 15-20% (volume pe depend)
- **Risk:** Impermanent Loss (price change se loss)

**Analogy:** Currency exchange booth jo khud chalata hai.

---

### 5. **MovePosition** (Liquidity Management)
**Kya Hai:** Advanced LP strategies, automated rebalancing.
- **TVL:** $8.7M
- **Focus:** Efficient liquidity provision

---

## üîÆ Oracles (Price Feeds)

### **Pyth Network**
Real-time asset prices blockchain par laata hai.
- **MOVE/USD Price:** Live market price
- **Why needed:** Smart contracts ko bahar ki duniya ka data chahiye

**Example:** Echelon ko pata hona chahiye ki 1 ETH = $3,500 hai (liquidation calculate karne ke liye).

## üìà Common DeFi Strategies

### 1. **Staking**
Tokens lock karke rewards kamao.
- **Risk:** Low
- **Return:** 5-15% APY

### 2. **Yield Farming**
Multiple protocols mein invest karke maximize returns.
- **Risk:** Medium-High
- **Return:** 10-50% APY

### 3. **Liquidity Provision**
DEX pool mein tokens dalo, trading fees kamao.
- **Risk:** Medium (impermanent loss)
- **Return:** 15-30% APY
